SeedRamp is a seed venture fund

We give you cash to cover the next calendar month (up to $20k), you give us stocks (up to 1%)

We make investment decisions in 24 hours, after a one-hour interview (schedule!)

Here is how it works. You schedule an interview and we meet somewhere in Palo Alto. You pitch, we ask, then we discuss. Then, we either give you a check or explain why we don't think your plan will work. We video record the entire session and publish it online.

We don't care what you do with the money. We don't sit on your board and we don't control you anyhow.

You can come back to us for more money, in a month, not earlier. Also, if we reject you, don't hesitate to come back in a month.
